Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General

Are my files and data safe with PDFTree?

Yes, your files and data are completely safe. PDFTree processes all your documents directly within your web browser. This means your sensitive files never leave your computer and are never uploaded to our servers. Your data remains 100% private and secure.

Why don't my files get uploaded to a server?

PDFTree utilizes advanced client-side JavaScript libraries that operate entirely within your browser. This design choice prioritizes your privacy and security by eliminating the need to transfer your files to an external server for processing.

Is PDFTree completely free to use?

Yes, PDFTree is completely free to use for all its features, including image to PDF conversion, text to PDF conversion, and PDF merging.

What is the maximum file size I can use?

Since all processing is done locally in your browser, the practical file size limit depends on your device's available memory and processing power. Very large files might take longer to process and could potentially cause browser performance issues.

What browsers are supported?

PDFTree is designed to work with modern web browsers that support the necessary JavaScript features, such as the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.

Merging PDFs

Does the "Merge PDF" feature have a limit on the number of files?

There is no strict limit on the number of PDF files you can merge. However, merging a very large number of large files might strain your browser's resources.

Will my merged PDF maintain the quality of the original files?

Yes, the "Merge PDF" feature combines your original PDF files without altering their content or quality.

Converting Images to PDF

What image formats are supported?

Currently, PDFTree supports JPG and PNG image formats for conversion to PDF.

Troubleshooting

Who can I contact if I run into an issue?

If you encounter any problems or have questions, please feel free to email us at contact@pdftree.org.
